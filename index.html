About US

We have been serving Biryani to the people of Karachi for almost 25 years, at the same location. With changing times and dynamics of the city, in 2020 we relaunched our services with a new name, better taste and a revamped place. However we still hold firmly to the values of our father when we first opened our doors in 1997, to provide quality food and do our jobs with full integrity.
Looking forward to serving one of the best biryani in the city that is famous for its love of food.
